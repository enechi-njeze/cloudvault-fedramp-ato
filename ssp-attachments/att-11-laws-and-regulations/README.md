## Purpose

This folder contains the Laws, Regulations, Standards, and Guidance inventory for CloudVault FHX. This attachment provides the complete legal and regulatory compliance framework that governs the system, documenting every applicable law, regulation, executive order, OMB circular, NIST publication, and federal guidance document that CloudVault FHX must comply with as a FedRAMP High system handling PHI, PII, FTI, and CUI.

## Applicable Laws, Regulations, and Standards

### Federal Laws

| Law | Citation | Applicability |
|---|---|---|
| Federal Information Security Modernization Act (FISMA) | 44 U.S.C. § 3551 et seq. | Primary governing statute for all federal information security |
| Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) | Pub. L. 104-191 | PHI protection, breach notification, administrative safeguards |
| Privacy Act of 1974 | 5 U.S.C. § 552a | PII protection, system of records notices (SORNs) |
| E-Government Act of 2002 | Pub. L. 107-347 | Privacy Impact Assessments, federal e-government |
| Internal Revenue Code (FTI) | 26 U.S.C. § 6103 | Federal tax information confidentiality and disclosure rules |
| Affordable Care Act (ACA) | Pub. L. 111-148 | Health exchange operations, data sharing with HHS |
| Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) | 18 U.S.C. § 1030 | Unauthorized access prohibitions |
| FedRAMP Authorization Act | Pub. L. 117-290 (2022) | FedRAMP statutory authority and JAB authorization |
