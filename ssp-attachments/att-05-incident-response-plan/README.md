## Purpose

This folder contains the Incident Response Plan (IRP) for CloudVault FHX. The IRP documents the procedures for detecting, reporting, analyzing, containing, eradicating, and recovering from security incidents affecting the system. It satisfies the FedRAMP High requirements under the IR control family (NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5 IR-1 through IR-10) and aligns with US-CERT reporting requirements under FISMA.

## Key IRP Components

A complete IRP for a FedRAMP High system must include:

- Incident classification taxonomy (PHI breach, FTI disclosure, CUI loss, system availability, etc.)
- US-CERT reporting timelines: 1 hour for major incidents, 4 hours for significant incidents
- HHS OCR breach notification procedures under HIPAA (60-day rule)
- IRS TIGTA notification procedures for FTI incidents under IRS Pub 1075
- Chain of custody procedures for incident evidence
- Roles: Incident Commander, ISSO/ISSM, CISO, Legal Counsel, Public Affairs, AO notification

## FedRAMP Requirement

IR-1 requires a documented incident response policy and procedures. IR-3 requires annual incident response testing. IR-6 requires incident reporting to US-CERT within required timeframes. A copy of this IRP must be submitted with the authorization package and updated within 30 days of any significant change.
