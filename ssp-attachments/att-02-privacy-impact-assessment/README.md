## Required PIA Contents (per E-Government Act, Section 208 and OMB M-03-22)

- Description of the information collected and why it is needed
- How the information will be used, accessed, shared, and stored
- Whether a System of Records Notice (SORN) is required and its status
- Opportunities for consent and individual redress
- How the system ensures data accuracy and quality
- Risk assessment: what privacy risks exist and how they are mitigated
- Privacy risk rating (Low / Moderate / High)
- Privacy Officer review and signature
- Date of publication (PIAs must be published publicly per E-Government Act)

## Key PIA Facts for CloudVault FHX

- PII types collected: name, date of birth, SSN (eligibility only), address, health record identifiers, insurance data, FTI elements
- Beneficiary population: approximately 890,000 individuals across 47 federal agencies
- Privacy risk rating: MODERATE (residual, after mitigations)
- SORN reference: FHDO-SORN-2024-003
- PIA published at FHDO.gov
