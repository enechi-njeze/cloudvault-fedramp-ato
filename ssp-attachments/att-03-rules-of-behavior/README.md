## Purpose

This folder contains the Rules of Behavior (RoB) document for CloudVault Federal Health Exchange (FHX) and supporting compliance records. Upload the RoB document and signature log here.

## Files to Upload Here

- CV-FHX-ROB-2025-001.docx (or .pdf) -- signed Rules of Behavior document
- RoB-Signature-Log.xlsx (or .pdf) -- log of all users who have signed, with dates and next-due dates
- RoB-Acknowledgment-Form.docx -- individual acknowledgment form template

## Required RoB Contents (per NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5, PL-4)

- Explicit statement of user responsibilities and expected behavior
- Description of prohibited actions (e.g., credential sharing, unauthorized data download)
- Consequences for violation (disciplinary, legal, access revocation)
- Requirement for annual re-acknowledgment
- Applicability to all users including contractors and agency personnel
- User signature/acknowledgment mechanism
- ISSO and System Owner review and approval

## Key RoB Facts for CloudVault FHX

- Required of all 1,847 authorized users (agency personnel and contractors)
- Annual re-acknowledgment required; non-compliant accounts suspended
- Current compliance: 1,847 of 1,847 users current (100% as of March 31, 2026)
- Violations may result in referral for prosecution under 18 U.S.C. § 641
